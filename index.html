Features

Built with serious training in mind, this home wall boasts a number of unique features.

Freestanding

Pick a spot, place, & start climbing.


Renter? Not into pouring concrete? Want to move it with you? Then this is your wall.

Adjustable Angle

Supports angles from 25º to 40º and everything in between.


The wall is attached to the kicker with hinges. When you get better at climbing or want to train at a different angle to keep things interesting, go ahead!

Textured Climbing Surface

Yes, that's right.
No feet? No Problem.
Use the wall.


Texturized using sand for a near gym-quality experience (primer, paint, sand, paint, paint).

MoonBoard Compatible

Built to these moon-board specs. This doesn't have to be a standard training board, but it can be one.


Additional t-nuts were added for more setting freedom.

Pickup & Assembly

Pick-Up

What do I need to pick up the wall?

  • 3-4 people
  • 2 wrenches
  • 1 truck
  • 1 phillips drill/driver

What kind of truck do I need?

I suggest this 15ft U-Haul truck

How does the wall come apart?

The wall comes apart into a few pieces using just 2 wrenches :

  1. Climbing surface (8' x 12'3") (1)
  2. 12ft 2x6 support pieces (2)
  3. 8ft 2x6 support pieces (2)
  4. Base assembly (1)

Optional: If the wall is too heavy, the plywood panels can be removed using a drill/impact driver with phillips-head attachment .

Reassembly

What will I need for reassembly?

A tall ladder, 3-4 people, 2 wrenches, & possibly a drill if you've removed the plywood panels.

FAQ

What?

What exactly does the wall come with?

Everything you need to get started with a home climbing wall!

  1. Climbing Wall, Supports, & Hardware
  2. ~90 holds + hardware
  3. Two volumes
  4. Two setter's wrenches (5/16" and 7/32")
  5. One crash pad
  6. Two tarps (protect it from the rain!)
  7. Stainless Steel Bolts
  8. Extra wood screws & grey paint
  9. 5 concrete pieces to help with levelling the ground
